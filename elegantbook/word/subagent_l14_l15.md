### 词根 ALI, ALTER[ULTER, ALTRU]

[添加别的东西 ad (= to, add) + ulter<alter (= other)]

[ˈeɪliəneit]
[使具备其他性质 ali (= other)]

### 词根 PREHEND, PREHENS[PREN, PREGN, PRIEV]

[ˌæprɪˈhensɪv]
[抓得很近的 ap<ad (= to, near) + prehens (= take)]

① apprehension 忧虑；逮捕；理解  ② apprehensible 可理解的  ③ apprehend 逮捕；忧虑；理解  ④ Officials are apprehensive about possible terrorist attacks.（官员们担心可能发生的恐怖袭击。）
