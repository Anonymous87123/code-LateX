卢沟桥位于天安门广场西南15公里处，横跨永定河，是北京现存最古老的多拱石桥。卢沟桥最初建成于1192年，1698年重建，由281根柱子支撑。每根柱子上都有一头石狮。这些石狮的头、背、腹部或爪子上都藏着更多的狮子。这些石狮生动逼真、千姿百态，是卢沟桥石刻艺术的精品。桥上的石狮不计其数，因而北京地区流传着“卢沟桥上的石狮子——数不清”的说法。卢沟桥不仅以其美学特征闻名于世，还被公认为石桥建筑史上的一座丰碑。

    \item （2025.6）天宫空间站（TiangongSpaceStation）是中国首个太空实验室，拥有110多立方米使用空间，可驻留三名宇航员，在距地球表面400-450公里的轨道上运行。天宫空间站已实施180多个科学研究与应用项目，涉及空间生命科学、太空医学、空间材料科学等领域。天宫空间站的研究成果在我国得到了广泛应用，产生了显著的经济效益。例如，太空育种创造的直接经济效益高达数千亿元。这不仅标志中国在航天技术上取得了巨大进步，也表明中国为全球的太空研究和应用做出了重大贡献。\\The Tiangong Space Station (TiangongSpaceStation) is China’s first space laboratory, with more than 110 cubic meters of usable space. It can accommodate three astronauts and operates in orbit 400–450 kilometers above Earth’s surface. It has carried out more than 180 scientific research and application projects, involving fields such as space life sciences, space medicine, and space materials science. Its research achievements have been widely applied in China and have produced significant economic benefits. For example, space breeding has generated direct economic benefits of up to several hundred billion yuan. This not only marks China’s tremendous progress in aerospace technology, but also shows that China has made major contributions to global space research and applications.
    \item （2025.6）粤港澳大湾区（Guangdong-HongKong-Macao Greater Bay Area）具有得天独厚的地理位置，拥有完善的交通基础设施和丰富的产业资源。大湾区是中国开放程度最高、经济活力最强的区域之一，在国家经济发展中具有重要的战略地位。大湾区不仅将建成充满活力的世界级城市群和具有全球影响力的国际科技创新中心，还将打造成适合工作、购物、旅游的优质生活圈。随着改革开放的不断深人，大湾区的建设将进一步推动区域经济发展。到2035年，大湾区将实现成为国际一流湾区的目标。\\The Guangdong-Hong Kong-Macao Greater Bay Area has a uniquely favorable geographical location, complete transportation infrastructure, and abundant industrial resources. It is one of China’s most open and economically dynamic regions, with an important strategic position in national economic development. It will not only be built into a vibrant world-class city cluster and an international science and technology innovation center with global influence, but also into a high-quality living circle suitable for work, shopping, and tourism. As reform and opening-up continue to deepen, its development will further promote regional economic growth. By 2035, it will achieve the goal of becoming an internationally first-class bay area.
    \item （2025.6）自古以来，中国的水资源北缺南丰，分布极不均衡。为了有效解决北方严重缺水问题，中国政府实施了南水北调工程（theSouth-to-NorthWaterDiversion Project）。这是一项跨区域配置水资源的宏大水利工程。历经数十年的规划与筹备，工程于2002年开工建设，分为东、中、西三条线路，总长4350公里，惠及人口将超过4亿。自2014年通水以来，工程向北方调水累计已超500亿立方米，为北方地区的人民提供了可靠的水资源，同时也极大地改善了这一地区的生态环境，促进了经济的可持续健康发展。\\Since ancient times, China’s water resources have been scarce in the north and abundant in the south, with extremely uneven distribution. To effectively solve the severe water shortage in the north, the Chinese government implemented the South-to-North Water Diversion Project. It is a grand water conservancy project for cross-regional allocation of water resources. After decades of planning and preparation, construction began in 2002. The project consists of eastern, central, and western routes, with a total length of 4,350 kilometers, and will benefit more than 400 million people. Since water delivery began in 2014, it has transferred over 50 billion cubic meters of water to the north, providing reliable water resources for people there, greatly improving the region’s ecological environment, and promoting sustainable and healthy economic development.
    \item （2025.12）尊老是中华民族的传统美德，深深植根于中国人的思想和行为中，是人们普遍遵守的行为规范和社会准则。在当今的中国，这种美德得到广泛传承。社会各界积极营造敬老助老的社会氛围。为老年人提供便利服务已成为社会共识，例如社区专门开设长者食堂，公共场所配置优先座位。政府还出台了一系列政策，为老年人的权益提供有力保障。尊老是社会和谐与发展的重要基石，有助于培育良好的社会氛围，推动整个社会文明进步。\\Respecting the elderly is a traditional virtue of the Chinese nation, deeply rooted in the thoughts and behavior of the Chinese people, and a code of conduct and social norm generally observed by people. In today’s China, this virtue is widely inherited and carried forward. All sectors of society actively create a social atmosphere of respecting and assisting the elderly. Providing convenient services for the elderly has become a social consensus; for example, communities specially set up senior dining halls, and public places provide priority seats. The government has also introduced a series of policies to provide strong protection for the rights and interests of the elderly. Respecting the elderly is an important cornerstone of social harmony and development, helping to cultivate a good social atmosphere and promote the civilized progress of society as a whole.

Since reform and opening-up, China has continuously increased investment in scientific research on its journey toward self-improvement and self-reliance, making new breakthroughs in fields such as information technology, artificial intelligence, and aerospace engineering. 5G networks cover the whole country, telemedicine is developing rapidly, the use of electronic payment leads the world, and the Chang’e Lunar Exploration Project demonstrates remarkable development in the aerospace field. The spirit of self-improvement and self-reliance has become a powerful driving force for advancing national modernization, inspiring the Chinese people to keep striving to realize the Chinese Dream of national rejuvenation.
    \item （2025.12）节俭（frugality）是中华民族的传统美德。在中国古代，生产力低下，先人们深知劳动成果来之不易，因而秉持节俭的理念。到了物质极大丰富的今天，中国人民依然坚持节俭的生活方式，体现了理性消费的生活理念和对劳动的尊重。近年来，中国政府持续加强节约型社会的建设，倡导“光盘行动”，减少食物浪费，提倡简约低碳的生活方式，推进资源循环利用，反对过度消费。节约型社会的建设有力地推动了中国经济的高质量发展。\\Frugality is a traditional virtue of the Chinese nation. In ancient China, productivity was low, and our ancestors knew well that the fruits of labor were hard-won, so they upheld the idea of frugality. Today, despite great material abundance, the Chinese people still adhere to a frugal lifestyle, reflecting the concept of rational consumption and respect for labor. In recent years, the Chinese government has continuously strengthened the building of a resource-conserving society, advocated the “Clean Plate Campaign,” reduced food waste, promoted a simple and low-carbon lifestyle, advanced resource recycling, and opposed excessive consumption. The building of a resource-conserving society has strongly promoted China’s high-quality economic development.
    \item （2024.12）北斗（Beidou）卫星导航系统的成功研制是中国自改革开放以来取得的一项重大科技成就。研发人员经过不懈努力，攻克了一系列技术难题，北斗系统最终实现了全球覆盖和高精度定位，使中国成为世界上少数几个独立拥有全球卫星导航系统的国家之一。北斗系统已广泛应用于交通运输、灾害救援、天气预报、公共安全等诸多领域。北斗系统现在已经在国际上得到广泛认可，开始为越来越多的国家和地区提供优质服务。\\The successful development of the Beidou Satellite Navigation System is a major scientific and technological achievement China has made since reform and opening-up. Through unremitting efforts, researchers overcame a series of technical difficulties, and the Beidou system finally achieved global coverage and high-precision positioning, making China one of the few countries in the world to independently possess a global satellite navigation system. The Beidou system has been widely applied in many fields, such as transportation, disaster rescue, weather forecasting, and public safety. It has now gained wide international recognition and has begun to provide high-quality services for more and more countries and regions.
    \item （2024.12）洋山港（Yangshan Port)是上海航运中心的重要组成部分，是中国第一个深水港，也是世界上规模最大的深水港之一。经过近20年的发展，洋山港已实现高度自动化。数字技术和人工智能的使用大大减少了用工成本和碳排放。自主研发的码头管理系统可以在百公里之外对大型设备进行远程操控。洋山港看上去一片繁忙，现场却见不到人工操作，而且能够24小时不间断运作。洋山港将不断发展，为把上海建成一个全球航运中心做出更大贡献。\\Yangshan Port is an important part of the Shanghai shipping center. It is China’s first deep-water port and one of the largest deep-water ports in the world. After nearly 20 years of development, Yangshan Port has achieved a high level of automation. The use of digital technology and artificial intelligence has greatly reduced labor costs and carbon emissions. Its independently developed terminal management system can remotely control large equipment from a hundred kilometers away. Yangshan Port appears very busy, yet no manual operations can be seen on site, and it can operate continuously 24 hours a day. Yangshan Port will continue to develop and make greater contributions to building Shanghai into a global shipping center.
    \item （2024.12）邀游太空历来是中华民族的梦想。2003年，神舟五号飞船发射成功，杨利伟成为第一个飞入太空的中国宇航员。2008年，神舟七号升空，翟志刚成为中国历史上首位进行太空行走的宇航员。近年来，中国航天进入创新发展“快车道”，太空基础设施建设稳步推进，中国空间站于2022年全面建成。中国航天事业的迅速发展在中华民族的历史上写下了辉煌一页，也为人类文明进步做出了巨大贡献。未来，中国探索太空的脚步将迈得更稳、更远。\\Traveling in space has always been a dream of the Chinese nation. In 2003, the Shenzhou-5 spacecraft was successfully launched, and Yang Liwei became the first Chinese astronaut to fly into space. In 2008, Shenzhou-7 was launched, and Zhai Zhigang became the first astronaut in Chinese history to conduct a spacewalk. In recent years, China’s space industry has entered the “fast lane” of innovative development, space infrastructure construction has advanced steadily, and China’s space station was fully completed in 2022. The rapid development of China’s space industry has written a glorious page in the history of the Chinese nation and made great contributions to the progress of human civilization. In the future, China’s steps in exploring space will be steadier and go farther.
    \item （2024.6）中国的传统婚礼习俗历史悠久，从周朝开始就逐渐形成了一套完整的婚礼仪式，有些一直沿用至今。如今的中式婚礼习俗已有很大变化，但婚礼庆典仍然十分隆重。婚礼场地经过精心装饰，以象征喜庆（jubilance）的红色为主色调，摆放着许多祝愿新人幸福的物件。在婚礼上，新人要拜天地（bow to Heaven and Earth），拜父母和相互对拜，然后设宴招待宾客，并向宾客敬酒致谢。今天，许多年轻人依然钟情于传统的中式婚礼，体验独特而美好的中国式浪漫。\\Traditional Chinese wedding customs have a long history. Since the Zhou Dynasty, a complete set of wedding rituals gradually took shape, some of which have been followed to this day. Today’s Chinese wedding customs have changed greatly, but wedding celebrations are still very grand. The wedding venue is carefully decorated, with red, symbolizing jubilance, as the main color, and many objects expressing wishes for the newlyweds’ happiness are placed there. At the wedding, the newlyweds bow to Heaven and Earth, bow to their parents, and bow to each other. Then they hold a banquet to entertain the guests and toast them to express their thanks. Today, many young people still have a fondness for traditional Chinese weddings, experiencing the unique and beautiful Chinese-style romance.
    \item （2024.6）中国盛产竹子，是最早开发利用竹资源的国家。竹子在中国分布广泛，品种丰富。竹子实用性强，用于生产和生活的许多方面，如筷子、桌椅的制作和桥梁、房屋的建造。中国人爱竹，自古以来就有无数文人以竹为主题，创作了绚丽多彩的文学和绘画作品。竹子主干（stem）笔直，象征正直的品格。竹子具有强大的生命力和适应能力，无论环境多么恶劣，都能够顽强生存，因而寓意坚韧不拔的精神。几千年来，竹子一直被视为中华民族品格的象征。\\China abounds in bamboo and is the first country to develop and utilize bamboo resources. Bamboo is widely distributed in China and comes in rich varieties. It is highly practical and is used in many aspects of production and daily life, such as making chopsticks, tables and chairs, and building bridges and houses. Chinese people love bamboo. Since ancient times, countless scholars and writers have taken bamboo as their theme and created splendid literary and painting works. The main stem of bamboo is straight, symbolizing an upright character. Bamboo has strong vitality and adaptability; no matter how harsh the environment is, it can survive tenaciously, thus implying the spirit of perseverance. For thousands of years, bamboo has been regarded as a symbol of the character of the Chinese nation.

Today, the practical function of fans has greatly weakened, but as a cultural symbol and an art form, fans still play an important role in traditional Chinese culture.

The government also encourages various social organizations to provide services for the elderly. With the joint efforts of the government and social organizations, the elderly will live happier lives.

Elderly people with limited mobility can also enjoy door-to-door meal delivery services. At the same time, China is actively exploring other elderly-care models, such as home-based and community-based elderly care, to ensure that all elderly people are cared for in old age.
    \item （2023.6）近年来,越来越多的中国文化产品走向全球市场,日益受到海外消费者的青睐。随着中国对外文化贸易的快速发展,中国文化产品出口额已持续多年位居世界前列,形成了一批具有国际影响力的文化企业、产品和品牌。数据显示,中国的出版物、影视作品、网络文学与动漫作品等在海外的销售量连年攀升。中国政府出台了一系列政策鼓励和支持更多具有中国元素的优秀文化产品走出国门,扩大海外市场份额,进一步提升中国文化的世界影响力。\\In recent years, more and more Chinese cultural products have entered the global market and have increasingly gained favor among overseas consumers. 使用逻辑：给定首句之后，开头段只需完成“承接背景、迅速定性、提出立场”三件事。以下句子不再按积极、危机或平衡分类，而是统一作为通用观点句使用，可根据题目替换主语或少量宾语。

\begin{enumerate}
    \item 角色必要： \colorbox{yellow}{plays a pivotal role in} personal advancement, \colorbox{yellow}{making} ... \colorbox{yellow}{a practical necessity}.
    \item 选必超越：\colorbox{yellow}{is no longer optional but imperative}, moving beyond...preference into a broader...that deserves...
    \item 重要需措：...\colorbox{yellow}{is not only integral to}...\colorbox{yellow}{but also calls for} concrete measures to ...
    \item 必需防线：indispensable, serving as a \colorbox{yellow}{reliable defense against} stagnation, obsolescence, and counterproductive habits.
    \item 解毒冲表：This approach \colorbox{yellow}{serves as an antidote} to impulsive choices, superficial ambition, and immediate gains.
    \item 源自优先：...\colorbox{yellow}{grows out of} foundational mastery. It must \colorbox{yellow}{take precedence} over ...
    \item 困境侵蚀：This appealing notion \colorbox{yellow}{captures a modern dilemma} where...while gradually weakening...
    \item 压力审视：...\colorbox{yellow}{is under growing pressure, which makes it imperative to cultivate} disciplined habits of scrutiny.
    \item 利用保卫：...\colorbox{yellow}{harness the benefits of} this development while safeguarding individual rights and ethical boundaries.
    \item 边缘替代：\colorbox{yellow}{mustn't inadvertently marginalize} those..., so accessible alternatives should be preserved alongside...
\end{enumerate}


\begin{note}
口诀：\textbf{角选重，防解源，困压利边。}
它对应 10 个 item：
\begin{enumerate}
  \item \textbf{角：} plays a pivotal role in \ldots making \ldots a practical necessity
  \item \textbf{选：} is no longer optional but imperative
  \item \textbf{重：} is not only integral to \ldots but also calls for concrete measures to \ldots
  \item \textbf{防：} serving as a reliable defense against \ldots
  \item \textbf{解：} serves as an antidote to \ldots
  \item \textbf{源：} grows out of foundational mastery / take precedence over \ldots
  \item \textbf{困：} captures a modern dilemma where \ldots
  \item \textbf{压：} is under growing pressure, which makes it imperative to cultivate \ldots
  \item \textbf{利：} harness the benefits of \ldots while safeguarding \ldots
  \item \textbf{边：} mustn't inadvertently marginalize \ldots / alternatives should be preserved
\end{enumerate}
你把开头段想成一个“小法庭陈述”：
\begin{enumerate}
  \item 先定性：这个东西不是普通选择，它有角色、有必要、有措施。所以是 \textbf{角、选、重}。
  \item 然后说它为什么值得支持：它能当防线，能当解药，但它不能凭空来，必须建立在根基上。所以是 \textbf{防、解、源}。
  \item 最后加入成熟立场：它也有困境和压力，所以要一边利用好处，一边守住边界，不能把弱势者排除出去。所以是 \textbf{困、压、利、边}。
\end{enumerate}
考场调用时，你不用想完整句子，只问自己三个问题：
\begin{enumerate}
  \item 我要不要把主题定成“必要”？\quad 角 / 选 / 重
  \item 我要不要说它有什么正面功能？\quad 防 / 解 / 源
  \item 我要不要显得观点成熟、有边界？\quad 困 / 压 / 利 / 边
\end{enumerate}
最实用的背法是直接背这一串：
\begin{enumerate}
  \item 角选重，防解源，困压利边。
  \item 角色必要，选必超越，重要需措；
  \item 必需防线，解毒冲表，源自优先；
  \item 困境侵蚀，压力审视，利用保卫，边缘替代。
\end{enumerate}
\end{note}

\begin{enumerate}

    \item 环境充斥：In an environment \textcolor{blue}{saturated with...}, emerging patterns are \textcolor{blue}{fundamentally altering how people..}.

    \item 同步发展：As...\textcolor{blue}{evolve in step with}...,...are reaching unprecedented levels.
    \item 适应渴望：\textcolor{blue}{reflects...adjustment to} accelerated change and \textcolor{blue}{a deepening yearning for} a stable sense of...
    \item 小众永久：What was once \colorbox{yellow}{met with hesitation as} a \colorbox{yellow}{marginal notion} has shifted from a \colorbox{yellow}{transient novelty} \textcolor{blue}{to a permanent feature of...}.

\begin{note}
\textbf{背景重述} 这一节不要背成 11 句，它只做一件事：把题目里的现象放回时代背景里，让读者觉得“这不是孤立事件，而是时代结构的一部分”。

你把 11 条压成 4 步：

\begin{enumerate}
  \item 铺背景：隐藏背景 / 环境充斥
  \item 见趋势：清晰可见 / 同步发展 / 适应渴望
  \item 变常态：小众永久 / 成为主流 / 从何看出
  \item 入生活：相关实践 / 嵌入生活 / 抽象具体
\end{enumerate}

口诀：隐环显同渴，暂主标织嵌具。

\begin{enumerate}
  \item \textbf{背：} emerged against a backdrop of \ldots beneath the seemingly \ldots surface of \ldots
  \item \textbf{环：} In an environment saturated with \ldots
  \item \textbf{显：} plainly visible across contemporary society
  \item \textbf{同：} evolve in step with \ldots
  \item \textbf{渴：} reflects adjustment to accelerated change and a deepening yearning for \ldots
  \item \textbf{暂：} shifted from a transient novelty to a permanent feature of \ldots
  \item \textbf{主：} moved from the margins to the mainstream of \ldots
  \item \textbf{标：} become a standard feature of \ldots as seen in \ldots
  \item \textbf{织：} been woven into everyday routines
  \item \textbf{嵌：} deeply embedded in contemporary routines and career trajectories
  \item \textbf{具：} abstract awareness is translated into concrete daily choices
\end{enumerate}

你把它想成镜头推进：

\begin{enumerate}
  \item 先拉远景：这个现象背后有时代背景和信息环境。
  \item 再给趋势：它在社会中清晰可见，并且和技术、制度、公众期待同步发展。
  \item 再写常态化：它从边缘走向主流，从短暂新鲜事变成稳定特征。
  \item 最后落到生活：它被编入日常惯例，并把抽象意识变成具体选择。
\end{enumerate}

考场调用时这样选：

\begin{enumerate}
  \item 想写大背景：背 / 环
  \item 想写社会可见性：显 / 同 / 渴
  \item 想写趋势常态化：暂 / 主 / 标
  \item 想写生活落点：织 / 嵌 / 具
\end{enumerate}

它的画面是：先看时代背景，再看社会趋势，再看主流化过程，最后看它怎样钻进日常生活。
\end{note}

\subsection{举例论证}

\begin{enumerate}
    \item 概念具象：\colorbox{yellow}{In concrete terms}, this logic appears when abstract ideals are \colorbox{yellow}{translated into} practice through...

    \item 过程拆解：\colorbox{yellow}{More specifically}, breaking an aim into stages can \colorbox{yellow}{convert} intention \colorbox{yellow}{into sustained} progress.

    \item 路径对照：\colorbox{yellow}{A case in point is}..., whose value lies \colorbox{yellow}{not in}...alone, \colorbox{yellow}{but in}...

    \item 条件支撑：\colorbox{yellow}{Notably}, the effect becomes durable only when...is reinforced by conditions that \colorbox{yellow}{preserve}...

    \item 多元协同：\colorbox{yellow}{As evidence of this principle}, ...can \colorbox{yellow}{coordinate across} actors, resources, and settings to...

    \item 优势整合：\colorbox{yellow}{A more convincing example is}..., where...\colorbox{yellow}{combines}...\colorbox{yellow}{with}...instead of isolating one factor from another.

    \item 风险阻断：\colorbox{yellow}{To illustrate this point}, timely adjustment can \colorbox{yellow}{prevent}...\colorbox{yellow}{from escalating into} wider costs.

    \item 兜底补充：\colorbox{yellow}{In concrete terms}, responsible arrangements \colorbox{yellow}{are exemplified by} supplementary channels that remain available when...

    \item 审慎落地：\colorbox{yellow}{Notably}, mature implementation appears when abstract caution is \colorbox{yellow}{translated into} concrete procedure.

    \item 反面验证：\colorbox{yellow}{By contrast}, when...is left unchecked, it may \colorbox{yellow}{amplify avoidable harm}. A disciplined response can...
\end{enumerate}

\begin{note}
\textbf{举例论证} 不要背成固定例子，而要背成 5 组论证动作。题目给什么材料，都先判断它承担哪一种证明任务：
\begin{enumerate}
  \item 落地类：概念具象 / 过程拆解
  \item 比较类：路径对照 / 条件支撑
  \item 协同类：多元协同 / 优势整合
  \item 保障类：风险阻断 / 兜底补充
  \item 校正类：审慎落地 / 反面验证
\end{enumerate}
口诀：具拆，对条，协合，阻兜，审反。

展开就是：

\begin{enumerate}
  \item \textbf{具：} In concrete terms, this logic appears when abstract ideals are translated into practice.
  \item \textbf{拆：} More specifically, breaking an aim into stages can convert intention into sustained progress.

  \item \textbf{对：} A case in point is \ldots not in \ldots alone, but in \ldots
  \item \textbf{条：} Notably, the effect becomes durable only when \ldots is reinforced by conditions that preserve \ldots

  \item \textbf{协：} As evidence of this principle, \ldots coordinate across actors, resources, and settings.
  \item \textbf{合：} A more convincing example combines \ldots with \ldots instead of isolating one factor from another.

  \item \textbf{阻：} To illustrate this point, timely adjustment prevents \ldots from escalating into wider costs.
  \item \textbf{兜：} In concrete terms, responsible arrangements are exemplified by supplementary channels.

  \item \textbf{审：} Notably, mature implementation appears when abstract caution is translated into concrete procedure.
  \item \textbf{反：} By contrast, when \ldots is left unchecked, it may amplify avoidable harm.
\end{enumerate}

你把它想成“不是背例子，而是给例子安排任务”：

\begin{enumerate}
  \item 抽象观点太空，就用\textbf{概念具象、过程拆解}。
  \item 想证明不是表面功夫，就用\textbf{路径对照、条件支撑}。
  \item 论据涉及多人、多资源、多场景，就用\textbf{多元协同、优势整合}。
  \item 担心失控或不公平，就用\textbf{风险阻断、兜底补充}。
  \item 想显得有思辨，就用\textbf{审慎落地、反面验证}。
\end{enumerate}

考场调用时不要先问“我有没有某个固定例子”，先问“这个例子承担什么论证功能”：

\begin{enumerate}
  \item 要把理念变实：具 / 拆
  \item 要把优劣讲清：对 / 条
  \item 要把合力写出来：协 / 合
  \item 要把边界补上：阻 / 兜
  \item 要把反面压住：审 / 反
\end{enumerate}

最短记忆版就背这一句：具拆，对条，协合，阻兜，审反。
它像一条论证流水线：先把观点变成画面，再拆开过程；接着比较路径、补足条件；然后写协同与整合；最后用风险阻断、兜底安排、审慎落地和反面验证把逻辑收紧。
\end{note}

\subsection{因此}

\begin{enumerate}
    \item As demanding routines and cultures of comparison become increasingly normalized, emotional strain, fragmented attention, and the erosion of authentic standards of personal worth \colorbox{yellow}{are becoming more apparent}.
    \item 重塑形式：traditional forms of...\colorbox{yellow}{have been fundamentally reshaped} by... that increasingly mediate...
    \item 模糊界限：boundaries across...\colorbox{yellow}{have been substantially blurred}.
    \item 重组形式：\colorbox{yellow}{are being reorganized around} more flexible and coordinated forms of...engagement.
    \item 转向而非：expectations \colorbox{yellow}{are shifting toward} demonstrable competence \colorbox{yellow}{rather than} passive familiarity with...
    \item 零星变多：\colorbox{yellow}{moves from} sporadic...\colorbox{yellow}{toward} organized and reliable participation.
    \item 除障使得：\colorbox{yellow}{systematically dismantle} communication barriers and \colorbox{yellow}{render cooperation more sustainable}.
    \item 渠道增多：\colorbox{yellow}{gain wider access to} necessary resources when modern systems remain both efficient and inclusive.
    \item 跟随标志：\colorbox{yellow}{It follows that}...\colorbox{yellow}{has become a visible marker of} maturity amid \colorbox{yellow}{a rising tide of}...
\end{enumerate}

\begin{note}
\textbf{因此} 这一节本质不是“举例”，而是写\textbf{前面现象带来的结果}。你把它背成一条变化链：

\begin{enumerate}
  \item 显现 $\to$ 重塑 $\to$ 模糊 $\to$ 重组 $\to$ 转向 $\to$ 扩大 $\to$ 除障 $\to$ 增渠 $\to$ 成标志
\end{enumerate}

口诀：

\begin{enumerate}
  \item 显重模组，转扩除渠标。
\end{enumerate}

对应 9 个按钮：

\begin{enumerate}
  \item \textbf{显：} are becoming more apparent
  \item \textbf{重：} have been fundamentally reshaped by
  \item \textbf{模：} have been substantially blurred
  \item \textbf{组：} are being reorganized around
  \item \textbf{转：} are shifting toward \ldots rather than \ldots
  \item \textbf{扩：} moves from sporadic \ldots toward organized and reliable participation
  \item \textbf{除：} systematically dismantle \ldots barriers and render \ldots more sustainable
  \item \textbf{渠：} gain wider access to \ldots when systems remain both efficient and inclusive
  \item \textbf{标：} It follows that \ldots has become a visible marker of maturity amid a rising tide of \ldots
\end{enumerate}

你把它想成一个城市改造过程：

\begin{enumerate}
  \item 先是问题越来越明显：\textbf{显}。
  \item 然后旧结构被改造：\textbf{重塑、模糊、重组}。
  \item 接着评价标准变了：\textbf{转向}。
  \item 再往外扩散：\textbf{零星变多、消除障碍、渠道增多}。
  \item 最后变成成熟社会的标志：\textbf{成标志}。
\end{enumerate}

考场调用时这样选：

\begin{enumerate}
  \item 写负面后果：显
  \item 写时代变化：重 / 模 / 组
  \item 写评价标准变化：转

  \item 想升格评价：义 / 评
\end{enumerate}

最短只背这一句：超应必，长防衡；双锚值，义评。

它的画面是：先把事情定大，再说明它有价值，再写它需要平衡，最后给出评价标准。
\end{note}

\subsection{行动分工}

\begin{enumerate}
    \item 个人落地：Individuals should \colorbox{yellow}{translate awareness into} disciplined routines and concrete conduct.
    \item 协同培育：Schools, families, and communities should \colorbox{yellow}{work in concert to nurture} sound judgment.
    \item 责任互补：Relevant stakeholders should \colorbox{yellow}{assume complementary responsibilities}, with individuals..., organizations..., and policymakers...
    \item 条件供给：Institutions should \colorbox{yellow}{create the enabling conditions for} responsible participation \colorbox{yellow}{to flourish}.
    \item 制度托举：Personal effort should be \colorbox{yellow}{reinforced by institutional safeguards} so that isolated enthusiasm can mature into...
\end{enumerate}

\begin{note}
\textbf{行动分工} 不要背成五句号召，它是一条责任扩散线：从个人开始，最后落到制度托底。

口诀：

\begin{enumerate}
  \item 个协责条托。
\end{enumerate}

对应 5 个按钮：

\begin{enumerate}
  \item \textbf{个：} Individuals should translate awareness into disciplined routines and concrete conduct
  \item \textbf{协：} Schools, families, and communities should work in concert to nurture sound judgment
  \item \textbf{责：} Relevant stakeholders should assume complementary responsibilities
  \item \textbf{条：} Institutions should create the enabling conditions for responsible participation to flourish
  \item \textbf{托：} Personal effort should be reinforced by institutional safeguards
\end{enumerate}

考场调用时这样写顺序：

\begin{enumerate}
  \item 先写个人：把意识变成习惯。
  \item 再写身边系统：学校、家庭、社区一起培育判断力。
  \item 再写各方分工：个人、组织、政策制定者各管一块。
  \item 最后写制度：给条件、给保障，让热情变成长期实践。
\end{enumerate}

最短只背这一句：个协责条托。

它的画面是：一个人先站起来，周围人一起帮，各方接力，制度在底下托住。
\end{note}

\subsection{约束机制}

\begin{enumerate}
    \item 原则锚定：\colorbox{yellow}{be anchored in} measurable accountability and enduring commitment \colorbox{yellow}{rather than reduced to} temporary enthusiasm or superficial compliance.
    \item 标准校准：Standards should \colorbox{yellow}{be recalibrated toward} verified quality rather than polished packaging or immediate indicators.
    \item 防止空转：Clear standards, regular evaluation, and enforceable responsibilities can \colorbox{yellow}{prevent} good intentions \colorbox{yellow}{from degenerating into} empty ritualism.
    \item 划定边界：A viable mechanism must \colorbox{yellow}{demarcate firm limits on} practices that allow efficiency to override dignity, fairness, or human agency.
    \item 监管识别：Institutions should \colorbox{yellow}{strengthen detection and enforcement against} manipulative incentives, exploitative design, and irresponsible amplification.
\end{enumerate}

\begin{note}
\textbf{约束机制} 是为了防止第四段变成空喊口号。它写的是：行动必须有边界、有标准、有纠偏。

口诀：

\begin{enumerate}
  \item 锚标防界查。
\end{enumerate}

对应 5 个按钮：

\begin{enumerate}
  \item \textbf{锚：} be anchored in measurable accountability and enduring commitment
  \item \textbf{标：} be recalibrated toward verified quality rather than polished packaging
  \item \textbf{防：} prevent good intentions from degenerating into empty ritualism
  \item \textbf{界：} demarcate firm limits on practices that override dignity, fairness, or human agency
  \item \textbf{查：} strengthen detection and enforcement against manipulative incentives
\end{enumerate}

考场调用时这样选：

\begin{enumerate}
  \item 怕形式主义：锚 / 防
  \item 怕只看表面：标
  \item 怕效率压倒人：界
  \item 怕平台或制度失控：查
\end{enumerate}

最短只背这一句：锚标防界查。

它的画面是：先把行动钉住，再校准标准，防止空转，划出边界，最后检查执行。
\end{note}

\subsection{愿景抬升}

\begin{enumerate}
    \item 风气成形：\colorbox{yellow}{As responsible practice becomes a shared norm}, a more coherent and resilient social climate will gradually take shape.
    \item 公共理性：A more mature public sphere will emerge when \colorbox{yellow}{rational scrutiny replaces} emotional polarization, restoring trust and preserving intellectual independence.
    \item 稳健适应：Individuals and institutions can \colorbox{yellow}{keep pace with} technological and social change \colorbox{yellow}{without sacrificing} judgment, dignity, or responsibility.
    \item 面向未来：Technology, institutions, and personal agency will function \colorbox{yellow}{as bridges rather than barriers}, enabling clearer identity and stronger resilience.
    \item 责任成熟：\colorbox{yellow}{Only through this balanced approach can} fragmented awareness evolve into sustained responsibility, allowing progress to retain substance, endurance, and moral clarity.
\end{enumerate}

\begin{note}
\textbf{愿景抬升} 不再写“应该怎么做”，而是写“这样做之后，会形成什么更成熟的状态”。

口诀：

\begin{enumerate}
  \item 风理适桥责。
\end{enumerate}

对应 5 个按钮：

\begin{enumerate}
  \item \textbf{风：} responsible practice becomes a shared norm
  \item \textbf{理：} rational scrutiny replaces emotional polarization
  \item \textbf{适：} keep pace with change without sacrificing judgment or dignity
  \item \textbf{桥：} function as bridges rather than barriers
  \item \textbf{责：} fragmented awareness evolves into sustained responsibility
\end{enumerate}

考场调用时这样想：

\begin{enumerate}
  \item 先写社会风气：责任成为共同规范。
  \item 再写公共理性：理性审视替代情绪对立。
  \item 再写稳健适应：能跟上变化，但不牺牲判断和尊严。
  \item 再写未来入口：技术、制度和个人能成为桥，而不是障碍。
  \item 最后写成熟状态：零散意识变成持续责任。
\end{enumerate}

最短只背这一句：风理适桥责。

它的画面是：风气成形，理性回归，稳健适应，桥梁打开，责任成熟。
\end{note}
